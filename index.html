DAMAI MEANS 'PEACEFUL'

The Damai has just 15 villas, set in one of the most beautiful gardens in Bali. Some have pools,
some have verandas. They all have views. And often the breeze carries the scent of cloves from
the nearby spice plantations. Simple luxuries. Graceful tropical living. The good things in life.
